Extra Activity For Your Fitness

We have all heard it many times before regular exercise is good for you, and it can help you manage your weight.

Swim

Swimming is a good all-round activity .because it: keeps your heart rate up but takes some of the impact stress off your body. builds endurance, muscle strength and cardiovascular fitness. helps you maintain a healthy weight, healthy heart and lungs.

Running

Running is a high impact, weight-bearing activity, which means that the rhythmic pounding of the pavement stresses your bones in a way that can be very healthy. Your bones respond to the stress by getting stronger in order to handle the recurring impact.

Cycling

BMI Calculator

Body mass index (BMI) is a medical screening tool that measures the ratio of your height to your weight to estimate the amount of body fat you have.

BMI calculator Chart

BMI Weight status
Below 18.5 Underweight
18.5-24.9 Healthy
25.0-29.9 Overweight
30.0-and Above Obese

Calculate Your BMI

Healthcare providers calculate BMI by using weight in kilograms (kg) divided by the square of height in meters (m2).
